Privacy Policy for OrgWallet: Financial Tracking and Forecasting App for Student Organizations Last updated: February 10, 2026

OrgWallet (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a financial tracking and forecasting platform designed for student organizations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App.

Information We Collect
While using OrgWallet, you may voluntarily provide limited personal information such as:

Name – used as an identifier for your records within the app

Email address – used only to associate records with a user profile

These identifiers are not verified and may be pseudonyms. No other personal or device information (such as contacts, photos, or location) is collected.

The app does not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising networks, or third-party tracking.

How We Use the Information
We use the information you provide solely to:

Identify and associate your records within the app’s internal database

Enable app functionality such as budget tracking and forecasting

Maintain your access to stored data

We do not:

Send marketing emails or notifications

Share, sell, or disclose data to any third party

Use information for analytics or advertising purposes

Data Storage and Security
OrgWallet uses Firebase, a Google service, for secure data storage and authentication. All communication with Firebase is encrypted via HTTPS. We do not store user information outside Firebase, and we rely on Google’s infrastructure for security and reliability.

Data Retention and Deletion
Your data remains in the system as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at jack.orgwallet@gmail.com , and your records will be permanently removed from our database.

Children’s Privacy
OrgWallet is intended for use by members of student organizations and is not designed for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available within the App or through the same link where this policy is published.

Contact Us
For any questions or data requests, please contact: jack.orgwallet@gmail.com
